Welcome to my Neighborhood!

I live in downtown Milwaukee, Wisconsin and it's great to be so close to restaraunts, shops, and grocery stores. I can walk to almost anywhere I want to go!

One of the best restaraunts for some fantastic middle eastern food is Casablanca!

Visit their website here!

During the summer, it's always great to relax at Cathedral Square Park where you can listen to Jazz in the Park.

Check out the schedule of the schedule of events before you go!


Some of the best places to visit while you're in the city include:

  • Visit the Milwaukee Public Art Museum
  • Take in a baseball or basketball game at either Miller Park or the Fiserv Forum
  • Enjoy a day of sun, sand, and swimming at Brady Beach
